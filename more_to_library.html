COMMUNITY LIBRARY

WHY IT MATTERS?

  • Supports education for underprivileged children.
  • Encourages women and youth to continue learning.
  • Promotes reading habits, digital literacy, and career awareness.
  • Builds a knowledge hub that strengthens the whole community.

Your support makes lifelong learning accessible and helps break the cycle of poverty through education.

HOW WILL THE FUNDS BE USED?

  • Renting or renovating a small community space.
  • Purchasing books (children’s books, exam guides, vocational skills, health awareness).
  • Setting up shelves, furniture, and lighting.
  • Adding computers and internet for digital learning.
  • Hiring/volunteering librarians or coordinators.

IMPACT

  • Every ₹1,000 donation provides 10 new books.
  • A single library can serve 500–1,000 people in a village/town.
  • Improves literacy rates and school performance.
  • Empowers women and youth with resources for skill development.

DONOR ENGAGEMENT

  • Donors get recognition on a “Wall of Supporters.”
  • Periodic updates with photos of children and families using the library.
  • Option to donate books directly.
