COMMUNITY PLANTATION DRIVE

Why It Matters?

  • Tackles climate change by increasing green cover.
  • Reduces air pollution and improves oxygen levels.
  • Provides shade, biodiversity, & healthier living spaces.
  • Engages schools, women & youth in sustainability.

How Funds Will Be Used?

  • Buying saplings (fruit trees + native species).
  • Soil preparation and manure.
  • Watering systems (drip irrigation / tanks).
  • Community awareness workshops.
  • Maintenance for at least 1–2 years.

Donor Engagement:

  • Progress updates with photos/videos of the plantation drives.
  • “Adopt a Tree” certificates for contributors.
  • Volunteer opportunities for plantation drives.

Impact:

  • Each ₹500 donation plants and nurtures 5 saplings.
  • Goal: 10,000 saplings across 20 villages.
  • Estimated 200 tonnes of CO₂ absorbed yearly.
  • Stronger community ownership of local environment.
